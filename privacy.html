Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how "Slime Squeeze Beat: Rhythm Game" (the "App") handles user information.

1. Information Collection

The App itself does not require users to enter personal information such as name, email address, or location.

2. Advertising (Including Third-Party Data Sharing)

This App uses Google AdMob, a service provided by Google, to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (IDFA / Advertising ID) and device information for the purpose of delivering and personalizing ads.

On iOS, an "Allow Tracking" permission dialog is shown the first time you open the App. Information is only collected for personalized ads if you grant permission. If you decline, non-personalized ads will be shown instead, and the App will continue to function normally.

For details on how Google AdMob handles data, please refer to Google's policies below:

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
How Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Information collected through the App is used solely for advertising delivery and for responding to inquiries regarding bugs or support requests.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

Except for Google AdMob as described in Section 2, we do not share user information with any third parties.
